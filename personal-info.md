# What I'm Building

## Lessi AI
Co-Founder & CTO

Lessi is an AI platform designed to help **special education teachers** create high-quality instructional materials tailored to the needs of every student.

The platform uses AI to generate and adapt:

- Lesson plans  
- Assessments  
- Reading passages  
- Instructional supports  

Content is aligned with:

- Individualized Education Programs (IEPs)
- State and national standards
- Evidence-based instructional practices

Lessi combines **AI generation, structured educational content, and teacher workflows** to dramatically reduce preparation time while improving instruction quality.

- Designing reliable systems

AI is accelerating development velocity, but it does not eliminate the need for strong engineering judgment.
